About Our School

Victor's School is committed to academic excellence and character development. We provide a safe and friendly learning environment where students can grow academically and socially.

Our goal is to help every student discover their talents and achieve success through quality education and practical learning.

Academics

We provide quality education that helps students grow academically, morally, and socially.

Subjects Offered

Mathematics, English Language, Basic Science, Civic Education, Computer Studies, Coding & Programming, Social Studies, and more.

Classes & Levels

We offer Nursery, Primary, and Secondary education with qualified teachers for each level.

Teaching Style

Our teaching methods are interactive, practical, and student-friendly to improve understanding.

Science Laboratories

Students perform practical experiments in well-equipped science laboratories.

ICT Training

We train students in computer operations, digital skills, and coding/programming.

Library

Our library contains educational books and learning materials for students and teachers.

Examination Success

Our students achieve excellent results in internal and external examinations.

Qualified Teachers

We have experienced and dedicated teachers committed to academic excellence.

Extracurricular Activities

Students participate in sports, debates, music, cultural events, and other activities.
